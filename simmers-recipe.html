Garlic Butter Pasta
The ultimate weeknight pasta — rich, garlicky, and done before you even realize you started cooking. Simple ingredients, big flavor.
15
Minutes
2
Servings
Easy
Difficulty
480
Calories
Ingredients
Spaghetti200g
Butter3 tbsp
Garlic cloves4 pcs
Parmesan2 tbsp
Fresh parsleyhandful
Salt & pepperto taste
Steps
1
Boil pasta in well-salted water until al dente, about 8 minutes. Reserve ¼ cup pasta water before draining.
2
Meanwhile, melt butter in a large pan over medium heat. Add minced garlic and cook for 1–2 minutes until fragrant — don't let it brown.
3
Add drained pasta to the pan and toss to coat. Splash in a little pasta water if needed to loosen. Season generously with salt and pepper.
4
Plate up, top with freshly grated parmesan and chopped parsley. Serve immediately.
