How it works

Turn your cooking videos into recipes in seconds.

1
Link your own YouTube video
Made a cooking video? Drop the link to your own YouTube video into Simmers to get started.
2
AI extracts the recipe
Our AI watches the video, pulls out all ingredients and steps, and formats them into a clean recipe card.
3
Share with the community
Post your recipe to your profile, add your own photos or videos, and let others discover your cooking.
4
Cook & get inspired
Browse your personalized feed, save recipes you love, and build your own digital cookbook over time.
